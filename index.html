Frozen Embers

Frozen Embers is a fun color scheme to make your coding and design workflow a bit more fun and exciting!

"made for the ones that care for their vision!"

for all

Frozen Embers was made for all kinds of people alike, web developer, back-end developer, machine-learning engineer, 'programmers', designers, artists. anyone can use it!

We care

We focus on your vision, we don't want anything happening to your precious eyes whilst you do your job, or something else. That's the sole reason of FE's existence!

Looks cool

It looks way cool than many other color schemes out there, trust us, we're not bragging! Anyways, if you don't believe us, go ahead, Try it yourself!

Hand-picked colors for you!

The Hand picked colors of frozen embers have passed the WCAG 2 Contrast and Color Requirements. It was created to care for your eyes. and not destroy them! The colors give you a fun vibe!

How I came up with FE..

I am a developer/designer, I have always wanted to make my coding workflow look better, get a new font, add some colors to it, ya' know, make it pretty! that's when I came across a color scheme called Monokai. I just loved it! years later, I shifted to a non-official variant called Monokai-Soda, since then I've always wanted to make a color scheme of my own!
